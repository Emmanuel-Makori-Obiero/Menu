Main Courses

  • Grilled Salmon

    Salmon fillet seasoned and grilled to perfection, served with lemon butter sauce.

  • Roasted Herb Chicken

    A reliable and classic choice that balances comfort and flavor. The chicken is typically seasoned with herbs like rosemary and thyme, then roasted with lemon and garlic until the skin is golden and crispy and the meat remains tender. It is frequently served with sides such as mashed potatoes or roasted seasonal vegetables.

  • Vegetable Samosas

    A globally beloved snack featuring a crispy, golden pastry shell shaped like a triangle. The interior is typically stuffed with a savory, spiced mixture of potatoes and green peas, seasoned with aromatics like ginger, garlic, and garam masala. They are usually served hot with a side of tangy tamarind or fresh mint chutney.

Desserts

  • Chocolate Fondue

    Assorted fruits and marshmallows served with rich chocolate fondue.

  • Warm Apple crumble

    A comforting, rustic dessert featuring a base of sliced, sweetened apples seasoned with cinnamon and nutmeg. It is topped with a generous layer of buttery, crumbly streusel made from oats, flour, and brown sugar, which is baked until golden and crisp. It is often served with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or a dollop of whipped cream.

  • Sticky Toffee Pudding

    An iconic British dessert known for its deep, comforting sweetness. It consists of a moist, sponge-like cake made with finely chopped dates, which is completely smothered in a warm, rich butterscotch or toffee sauce. It’s traditionally served with vanilla custard or ice cream.
